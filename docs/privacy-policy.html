Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Budget Tracker stores all of your information on your own device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal or financial data. There is no account to create and no server that receives your data.

Who we are

Budget Tracker ("the app") is developed and published by Break and Build ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains how the app handles your information.

What information the app handles

The app lets you record budgets, income, priorities, savings, categories, and transactions. All of this information is:

  • Entered by you directly into the app.
  • Stored only on your device, using your device's local storage.
  • Never sent to us or to any third party. The app has no backend server and makes no network requests that transmit your data.

Information we do NOT collect

The app does not collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials (the app has no sign-in).
  • Your location.
  • Your contacts, photos, or files.
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs.
  • Usage analytics or crash logs sent to us.
  • Any financial account or bank information (the app is manual-entry only and does not connect to banks).

Notifications

If you choose to enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device (for example, a daily check-in and a month-end reminder). These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No notification content or schedule is sent to us or to any external service. You can turn reminders off at any time in the app's Settings.

Data storage and deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, you are always in control of it. You can delete it at any time from within the app (Settings → Reset), or by uninstalling the app, which removes all of its locally stored data.

Children's privacy

The app is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Future features

We may, in a future version, offer an optional cloud backup or sync feature so you can move your data between your own devices. If and when we do, it will be strictly opt-in, and we will update this Privacy Policy to describe exactly what is stored and how before that feature is made available.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
